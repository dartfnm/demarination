 ...... welcome to the Multimatograf 2024 party! ........
 finally, after 6 years of hiatus, we are back with a brand
 new portion of true oldskool demoparty!
   - brand new partyplace in Vologda, Russia
   - wide range of platforms - from ZX/BK to newskool
   - 15 awesome compos (including realtime)
   - seminars, lectures, exhibitions and live stream
   - remote entries welcome!

 join us at https://events.retroscene.org/mf2024 !

 :: requirements and notes
  - min. OCS chipset, 512kb chip + 512kb any
  - AGA or accelerated may need boot w/o startup sequence
    or disabling the accelerator
  - tested on A500, A600 and A1200/060
  - LMB to exit the intro at any time
  - try to find a hidden game!
